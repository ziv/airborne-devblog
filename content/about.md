My name is Ziv. I am a software engineer with a passion for flight simulators and a huge fan of the MicroProse titles I
grew up on.

While my day job is as a Principal Architect at ZoomInfo, at night I write code in low-level languages and expand my
knowledge of physics and mathematics.

![F-15 Strike Eagle II fr](/f15-se-ii.png)

I spent so many hours flying F15 Strike Eagle II on my XT-16MGhz machine with monochrome CGA resolution screen. I tried to play
it again, it was fun but somthing is missing. No, I don't want another DCS neither an arcade game, I want the same
gameplay I
got from this magic game, so this project has begun.



I created this blog to document the development of my flight simulator and to share the lessons I learn along the way.

I had 3 goals when I started this project:

1. Not to use a game engine.
2. To re-learn C++ and linear algebra.
3. To have fun.

Let's see how it goes :)

[It starts here](https://ziv.github.io/airborne/posts/001-getting-started/)
